meet the charity

  • Fraternity Without Borders is a charity registered in the UK caring for one of the poorest regions of the world: Mozambique. We have been working on the ground since 2009 where hunger, misery and helplessness make new innocent victims every day.

  • A selection of photographs showcasing Fraternity without Borders’ work will be exhibited from 20-29th March 2018 at the Brazilian Embassy in London. The charity has also been invited to participate in a fashion show in London on 23rd March 2018.

  • All funds raised through online auctions and donations will be invested in our social projects in Mozambique.

  • Click on the link below to get to know more about our work:

  • https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ebVg1j6_Zc4

meet the graffiti artist

  • Gustavo Nenão is an internationally renowned street artist. His career spans over 24 years with projects registered in 23 countries. His career highlights include a joint exhibition in NY by invitation of the former American first lady Michelle Obama. His clients include the football player Thierry Henry and the Brazilian celebrities Chiquinho Scarpa, Xuxa, Marcelo D2, Fabio Porchat, Ana Maria Braga and many more.

  • https://www.gustavonenao.com/clipping

  • Nenão will be performing a live graffiti art on canvas during the exhibition opening, which will live streamed on the 20th March from 6-10pm UK time. He will also perform live in a fashion show for Not Just Yoga in London on the 22rd March 2018 from 7-10pm.

  • http://notjustyoga.co.uk/travel-in-style-2/

  • The canvasses produced in both events will be on auction online and all funds raised will be donated to Fraternity without Borders.

meet the photographers

  • Alisson Demetrio is a professional music photographer and the official photographer of the internationally acclaimed DJ Alok.

  • His career highlights include clicks for the DJ David Guetta and Armin Van Buuren and many music festivals, including Tomorrowland in Belgium, UltraMusic in the US and the biggest Brazilian festivals such as Tribe, Tribal Tech, Lollapalooza, VillaMix, Planeta Atlântida and more.

  • http://www.alissondemetrio.com/

  • Alisson travelled to Mozambique in an official tour with DJ Alok for Fraternity without Borders. A selection of his work will be on show at the photography exhibition in London.

  • His work will be on auction online. 100% of the funds raised will be donated to Fraternity without Borders.

meet the photographers

  • Felipe Torres and Fernanda Calixto are amateur photographers from Brazil and sponsors of Fraternity without Borders. Their work presents a heart-warming portrait of the realities of Mozambique as well as a message of hope from the work in progress by Fraternity without Borders.

  • The showcase of their beautiful and very personal images is our homage to the hundreds of volunteers who travel to Africa in our humanitarian missions every year. Without them it would have been impossible to feed and support more than 9000 children, adults and elderly people.

  • A selection of Felipe and Fernanda’s work will be on show at the photography exhibition in London. All photographs will be on sale in an online fundraising auction for Fraternity without Borders.
